# KMI Gamers Website

Welcome to the **official repository for the website of Kerliix Multiplayer International Gamers** — the gaming division of **Kerliix Corporation**.  

Our mission is simple:  

> **Play. Connect. Compete.**  

KMI Gamers is all about building a dynamic community of players, streamers, and gaming innovators, powered by Kerliix’s technology ecosystem.

---

## About the Website

The **KMI Gamers website** is the central hub for all things gaming within the Kerliix ecosystem. It provides:  

- Community engagement and forums  
- Latest news and tournaments  
- Access to game-related tools and APIs for developers  
- Event and streaming integration  

Whether you’re a casual player, competitive gamer, or a developer building gaming experiences, KMI Gamers connects you to everything gaming under the Kerliix umbrella.

---

## Purpose

The website serves as the **primary gateway** to KMI Gamers, enabling:

- **Players** — Discover games, join tournaments, and connect with other gamers  
- **Streamers & Creators** — Share content, broadcast live, and grow your audience  
- **Developers** — Access gaming APIs, tools, and integrations at [dev.gamers.kerliix.com](https://dev.gamers.kerliix.com)  

Our goal is to create an environment that is **fun, collaborative, and technologically seamless**.

---

## Key Sections of the Site

- **Homepage**  
  Overview of KMI Gamers’ mission, featured games, and community highlights.

- **About**  
  Learn about our vision, core values, and the Kerliix gaming ecosystem.

- **Community**  
  Forums, discussion boards, and event calendars for all gamers.

- **Tournaments & Events**  
  Stay updated with competitive gaming events and online tournaments.
